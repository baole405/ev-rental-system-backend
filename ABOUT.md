# EV Station-based Rental System

## Overview
The **EV Station-based Rental System** is a software platform designed to manage the rental and operation of electric vehicles (EVs) at designated rental stations.  
It allows users to **register, rent, use, and return vehicles**, while providing station staff and system administrators with the tools to manage fleets, verify customers, track transactions, and analyze operational data.

The system supports **three main roles**:
1. **EV Renter (Customer)** – End-user who rents and uses EVs.
2. **Station Staff** – Personnel responsible for on-site operations, verification, and maintenance.
3. **Admin** – Central management overseeing all stations and business analytics.

---

## System Operation Flow

### 1. EV Renter Operations

#### a. Registration & Verification
- The renter creates an account and uploads necessary documents (driver’s license, ID card).
- Identity is verified online or directly by station staff.

#### b. Booking & Reservation
- The renter locates available EV stations on the map.
- They can view available vehicles (by type, battery level, range, price, etc.).
- The renter can **book in advance** or **rent directly on-site**.

#### c. Vehicle Pickup
- The renter **checks in** at the counter or via the app.
- They **sign an electronic rental contract**.
- Staff verifies the condition of the vehicle and confirms the handover (photo + checklist).

#### d. Vehicle Return
- The renter returns the vehicle to the same or an approved station.
- Staff inspects and confirms the vehicle’s condition.
- The system calculates **rental fees** and **additional charges** (e.g., overtime, damages).

#### e. Personal Dashboard & Analytics
- Renters can view trip history, cost breakdown, and performance analytics (distance, duration, etc.).

---

### 2. Station Staff Operations

#### a. Manage Handovers
- Staff views the daily schedule of rentals and returns.
- They inspect and update vehicle conditions.
- Confirm vehicle handover electronically with the renter.

#### b. Customer Verification
- Check customer’s driving license and ID.
- Match with online registration data for security.

#### c. On-site Payments
- Record rental fees and deposits directly at the station.

#### d. Station Fleet Management
- Update battery status, maintenance needs, or repair logs.
- Report technical issues to the Admin dashboard.

---

### 3. Admin Operations

#### a. Fleet & Station Oversight
- Monitor station performance and vehicle availability.
- Assign vehicles or staff as needed based on demand.

#### b. Customer and Staff Management
- Maintain customer risk profiles and staff accounts.
- Review customer feedback or violation reports.

#### c. Business Analytics
- View operational reports: rentals per station, peak times, revenue, vehicle usage rate.
- Analyze **upgrade demands** and forecast fleet expansion.
